Privacy policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. We’ve written it in straightforward language for an international audience.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information (e.g., name, email address, phone number) — provided voluntarily through forms, contact requests, or newsletters.

Project Information — details you share with us regarding your brand, business, or project requirements.

Usage Data — such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site (collected automatically via cookies or analytics tools).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to process applications, schedule interviews, and communicate updates. Your story (with your consent) may be featured in our show and promotional materials. We may use anonymous analytics (like Google Analytics) to improve our site experience.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We may share your submitted content with our production partners (Nereion Collective, Crown Client Management, AI Orbi) to create the show. Any third-party service we use (for analytics or hosting) is bound to protect your privacy.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and gather usage statistics. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, no online system is 100% secure. Please share sensitive details only through our official channels (like the Apply form or secure email).

6. Your Rights

You can unsubscribe from our emails at any time by clicking the link in the message. If you want us to delete your personal data, email us at [email protected] and we will comply in accordance with applicable laws (including GDPR for EU residents).

7. International Users

We operate globally. When you submit information, it may be stored and processed in countries where our partners (e.g. data centers in the EU, US, UAE) are located. We will handle it under data protection standards like GDPR, CCPA, and others as required.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our site is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has given us information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Updates to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The latest version will always be on this page with the date. By continuing to use the site after changes, you agree to the new terms.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about privacy, email Contact (at) thereallifeavengers.com We take your trust seriously and will respond promptly. Thank you for joining us on this journey while we protect your privacy every step of the way.
