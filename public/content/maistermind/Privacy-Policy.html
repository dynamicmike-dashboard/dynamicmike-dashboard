1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which mAIstermind ('we,' 'us,' or 'our') collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information gathered from clients and their prospects (end-users) in connection with our specialized Hyper-Personalization Fulfillment Service. By engaging with our service or visiting our landing pages, you agree to the terms outlined below. Our core service involves the use of AI to generate personalized video assets and landing pages based on data you provide, and this policy is designed to uphold the highest standards of data integrity and client trust

2. Information We Collect

A. Information Clients Provide To Us (Critical for Service)

We collect data necessary to fulfill our **mAIstermind** personalized video service. This includes:

  • **Personal Data:** Names, Email Addresses, and contact information for the client's team (for Walk-Through scheduling and communication).
  • **Prospect Data (Client's List):** CSVs or API inputs containing prospect information such as First Name, Company Name, Prospect LinkedIn URL, and any specific data points required for video personalization.
  • **Media Assets:** The base video and voice recordings provided by the client's spokesperson for lip-sync and voice-cloning purposes.

B. Automatically Collected Data (Via Landing Pages)

When prospects view the personalized landing pages we create for our clients, we automatically collect engagement data for analytics and billing purposes:

  • IP Addresses and Geolocation (to prevent abuse and track origin).
  • Video View Metrics (View percentage, clicks on CTA buttons).
  • Browser and Device Information.

We use the data strictly for:

  • Generating and hosting the unique, personalized videos and landing pages.
  • Billing our clients based on video generation volume.
  • Providing data/analytics back to our clients regarding their campaign performance.

    Data Storage, Security, and Retention

      "We are committed to securing the integrity of the valuable prospect data provided by our clients.
