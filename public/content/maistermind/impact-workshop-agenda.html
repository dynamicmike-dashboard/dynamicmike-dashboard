mAIstermind Workshop Agenda


This workshop is designed to be a fast-paced, high-value 60-minute session that will revolutionize your approach to content creation and lead generation using advanced AI.


Part 1: The $100K Assumption Mistake (5 minutes)

  • Hook: Why most entrepreneurs are leaving money on the table with their content.

  • Problem: Generic content and assumptions about your audience lead to wasted effort and missed conversions.

  • Solution Tease: How to identify and avoid common pitfalls using a "mind-reading" approach.


Part 2: Advanced AI Psychology Frameworks - The IMPACT Formula (20 minutes)

    • Introduction to The IMPACT Formula: A proprietary framework for deep audience insight.

      • I - Intent: Understanding user goals and desires.

      • M - Motivation: Tapping into emotional drivers.

      • P - Pain Points: Uncovering hidden struggles.

      • A - Aspirations: Identifying dreams and future desires.

      • C - Challenges: Recognizing obstacles and limiting beliefs.

      • T - Transformation: Articulating the desired outcome.

    • Live Demonstration: Applying the IMPACT Formula with AI (e.g., ChatGPT) to generate profound customer insights.

    • Ethical Guidelines: Ensuring authentic and responsible AI use; no fake testimonials or made-up stats.


Part 3: Multi-Format Content Multiplication (15 minutes)

    • Concept: Repurposing one core insight into various engaging content formats.

    • Strategy: How to generate captions, images (ideas), and video ideas in minutes using AI.

    • Live Examples:

      • Turning a core insight into a compelling short-form video script (Reels/TikTok).

      • Developing engaging social media captions.

      • Brainstorming visual concepts for posts.

    • Platform-Specific Bonuses: Mentioning tools like Napkin.ai for graphics and video optimization techniques.


Part 4: The Golden Goose Opportunity (10 minutes)

    • Problem: Valuable social media interactions often fail to convert into leads.

    • Solution Tease: A game-changing strategy to identify and capture engaged audience members.

    • Mechanism: Turning comments and likes into tangible business growth.

    • Call to Action Tease: Connecting engagement directly to your automation solution (without revealing specifics yet).


Part 5: Strategic Software Integration & Implementation (5 minutes)

    • Seamless Transition: Introducing your automation software as the natural next step.

    • Benefits: How the software streamlines the content process from insight to lead.

    • Real-time Value: Demonstrating how AI-powered automation saves time and maximizes impact.

    • Offer Reveal: Presenting the exclusive workshop offer (e.g., trial, discount for attendees).


Part 6: Q&A and Next Steps (5 minutes)

    • Open Floor: Addressing participant questions.

    • Resource Vault Access: Providing access to all prompts and frameworks from the presentation.

    • Community Building: Introducing the private alumni group for accountability.

    • Follow-Up Scheduling: Offering individual calls for software subscribers.

    • Recording Access: Sharing the workshop recording for review.
