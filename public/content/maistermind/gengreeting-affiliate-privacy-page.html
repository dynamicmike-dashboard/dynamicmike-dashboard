GenGreetings Affiliate Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025


This Privacy Policy explains how mAIstermind Ltd (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects and processes information about affiliates participating in the GenGreetings Affiliate Programme.


1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, and payment information provided through the Partnero platform.
  • Tracking data, including referral links and cookies that record visits and conversions for attribution purposes (90-day duration).
  • Technical data such as IP address and browser type, collected by analytics tools to improve performance and detect fraud.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To manage your affiliate account and calculate commissions.
  • To pay commissions and handle invoicing.
  • To provide support and send essential programme updates.

3. Data Sharing

We use Partnero to manage affiliate tracking and payments, and Stripe for subscription processing. Both are GDPR-compliant third parties. No personal data is sold or shared beyond these service providers.


4. Cookies

Cookies are used solely to track referrals and ensure commissions are attributed correctly. These cookies expire after 90 days unless renewed through a new click.


5. Data Retention

Affiliate data is retained for as long as your account is active and for up to seven years thereafter for tax and accounting compliance.


6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data at any time by contacting privacy (at) maistermind.com
