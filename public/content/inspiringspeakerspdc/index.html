ABOUT US

We are a group of enthusiastic members of the recently-founded English-speaking Toastmasters club in Playa Del Carmen.

We welcome everyone with or without public speaking experience, and our main intention is to boost your confidence and communication skills.


https://www.toastmasters.org/Find-a-Club/28677134-inspiring-speakers-pdc

Toastmasters International established 100 years ago (1924) and now has active clubs and members throughout the world.

We are Inspiring Speakers PDC Toastmasters Club!

We are proud to be the Yucatán’s Peninsula’s First English-speaking Toastmasters club!

What's Involved?

Our meetings follow a standard Internationally proven recipe.
We always aim to start and finish promptly, so please aim to arrive in good time.

Generally, the Club President will welcome everyone, and then introduce a Toastmaster for the evening.

The roles for Toastmaster and all meeting roles are revolved among the paid members who volunteer to take responsibility for one of the tasks.
So the Toastmaster will usually invite you to introduce yourself along with a thought on the theme of the week.
Then the Toastmaster will introduce the General Evaluator who will introduce the rest of the team to share what their roles entail.
This will include the Table Topics Master who will invite some attendees to speak for up to two minutes on a theme that is announced on the spot.
After a short comfort-break, we will usually hear from two member prepared speeches, followed by some evalution feedback and summaries from each of the roles.
