Another creative initiative from Mike Berry (DynamicMike)

Playa Photography & Videography Club


The club is open to everyone in Playa Del Carmen interested in photography and videography, including Make-Up Artists and Models.

We don't mind if you are a world-recognised professional, an enthusiastic amateur,
or simply want to take better photos with your smartphone...
all are welcome in every genre.

We have planned a number of outings and talks as well as weekly challenges.

As a result of setting up this club, we have already attracted a number of photographic and videography enquiries,
and club members will be given first priority to apply for those appointments.
