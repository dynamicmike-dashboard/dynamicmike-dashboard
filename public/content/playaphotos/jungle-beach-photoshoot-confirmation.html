Any images restrictions?

Normally there are significant photographic restrictions that will insist on the images retaining a watermark and credit given to the photographer with each use.
Indeed, many professional photographic services insist that you pay for every use.

We will not restrict your use in any way, including the ability to use or sell your own photographs for commercial purposes and any other use that you desire.

Although it is accepted that the copyright of the images and the RAW files, plus any editing, remains with the photographer, this photoshoot will grant you lifetime authorisation to use your images for any purpose.

You will receive a minimum of 30 images cropped and edited to look their best (in our opinion) that we hope you will be extremely proud of.
