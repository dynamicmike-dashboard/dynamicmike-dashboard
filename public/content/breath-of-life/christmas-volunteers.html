Christmas 2025

The largest Children's Christmas Party for the local community families in the Playa Del Carmen area.

Your contributions, donations and volunteering time are all very much appreciated.

VOLUNTEERS IMPORTANT

Please complete the volunteer registration here
Your wristband will be available to collect on arrival.

plus stay updated via the WhatsApp chat here

Main event dates this year are;
Saturday 20th December 2025
+
Saturday 3rd January 2025

This year we anticipate more than 350 Children, plus their families.
It is our intention to give gifts to every child, plus food, drinks and entertainment for everyone.

We hope to attract 100 volunteers to help on the day,
plus there are many more ways to contribute and donate in any way you can.

Volunteer Stations

* Security: all who doesn't have any specific task assigned as you see there's need in several activities and along the party.

* ice cream - - 3 people

* Vehicles - as many as we can get, for transportation of things and for rides for volunteers

* cleaning - as many as we can, we have 3 brooms and 3 dust pans, if you can bring another set it would be amazing.

* nails - - 2 people, planned by a volunteer.

You will be presented to your coordinator at arrival and we divide in teams from them. Then... ENJOY!!! ✨🫂💜

Agenda for the Day

🚗 Volunteers with vehicles: 1:00 PM
Meet at BreathOfLife to help transport items and equipment.

🧹 Cleaning team: 1:45 PM
To start preparing and cleaning the dome.

⏰ General volunteer arrival: 2:00 PM
Earlier is even better if you can!

We’ll need help cleaning the dome, setting up chairs, stalls, water, gifts, and activity areas.

🐶 Please leave pets at home
Just for these few hours — we want your full energy with the children and families.

🎒 Personal belongings
We will have a designated area for bags, but please stay mindful as usual.

🎟️ Volunteer identification
You’ll receive a special wristband for quick identification.

We will also try to provide simple name labels (even handwritten).

🤝 Buddy system
Please pair up with another volunteer to check on each other, stay hydrated, and take short breaks when needed.

🌈 Children’s wristbands
Each child will receive a colored wristband on entry. It will be cut off when they receive their gift.

🎉 Most important: STAY SAFE & HAVE FUN
Our intention is for everyone — kids, families, and volunteers — to enjoy uplifting energy, joy, smiles, and a truly memorable celebration.

Updated Schedule for December 20th 2025

1:00 PM – Volunteers with vehicles meet at BreathOfLife

2:00 PM – General volunteer arrival & setup
2:55 PM – Pastor Michele gathers volunteers for a few words
3:00 PM – Children arrive and receive wristbands
3:00–4:30 PM – Party games, activities, piñatas, entertainment
4:30 PM – Food break (pizza, empanadas, etc.)
5:00 PM – Gabbylow show
5:30 PM – Children line up by age group to receive gifts
6:00 PM – Final clean-up (all help appreciated!)
