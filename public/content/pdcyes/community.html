OUR VISION

Our vision is simply to continue growing, learning, sharing and loving the abundance of life...

We have so many talented individuals here in Playa, and so every month, we aim to introduce another gifted individual who is happy to share their wealth of life skills, education, knowledge, and experiences.

We want you to bring your joy along with you too, and share the delicious high vibrations with like-minded friends.

Community Assistance

Every month, Mike has endeavoured to introduce a new Local Community project for the public awareness, and opportunity to get involved.

Projects include;

Saving the stingless bees,

Planting trees,

Clearing litter off the beach,

Growing food,

Helping the orphaned dogs and homeless cats,

Fund-raising bazaars and goodie-collections for the local community families,

Teaching English (including to the local police force)

Christmas parties for the local community children and their families,

Regular distribution of food parcels to those in need

Supporting local musicians (including the blind who have very little other methods of generating income)


Add it to your diary now - PDCYES group events happen only once per month, and are FREE to attend - don't miss out.
