PDCYES Sunday 2nd March 2025 1pm-3pm 

For everyone interested in Personal Development in the Playa Del Carmen area.

Graham Jack - What's the problem?

Graham is a serial entrepreneur and investor who has a healthy fascination with the current global monetary and banking system and how it impacts all of us in more ways than you can imagine. And how you can empower yourself to take back control.

Ex-Architect turned investor and entrepreneur. Graham has spent over 8 years studying the monetary system and the societal impacts of global monetary policy.

The talk will not be overtly Bitcoin based but will describe an imaginary world where we have 2 systems - a broken monetary system and a functional one.

Javier Serrudo

Javier Serrudo is a Full Stack Developer specializing in decentralized systems, with expertise in Web3 architecture and asset tokenization.

With over eight years of active engagement in the cryptocurrency market, he drives technical education and implementation for individuals and businesses aiming to integrate cryptocurrency solutions into their operational or commercial ecosystems.

Additionally, he leads an artificial intelligence company focused on marketing and intelligent agents, with a core emphasis on leveraging asset tokenization to enhance decentralized applications and strategies.
