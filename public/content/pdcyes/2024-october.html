Jacqueline Fracassi - Slim Spa

  I am happy to share the story behind Slim Spa ( which is Slim MEDI Spa in Ave Constituyentes and Slim Body & Face Aesthetics on the 5th Ave).

 My name is Jacqueline (Jackie ) and I started this journey 20 years ago when I was left with two kids and nothing else due to a bad divorce when I lost all I had built in my young years even before my kids.

 I went back to Switzerland to study cosmetology, a completely different career than I had before (I was an international tour guide) so that my kids would have a free excellent elementary school we could live with my parents. 

I worked and studied to save up some money, and when the time was right, we returned to Cancun Mexico where I opened a little office in Mercado 28. 

After two years of working very hard, I saved up enough to rent a space in a new upcoming shopping mall in Cancun and opened my first Spa. As it happens, that was Bad timing... the day I had my opening, Mexico shut down due to the bird flu.

Fortunately, I had a proper financial emergency fund and I could survive.

I worked 7 days a week and slowly and surely I was blessed to create a successful business that I expanded to Puerto Morelos, Playa del Carmen and Tulum. 

With the main focus offering services with the most advanced medical technologies that would cost 4 times more in Canada, USA and Europe, and that is mostly our market. 

The COVID chaos made me shut down all of our branches except the two in Playa Del Carmen where I decided to relocate to. So no more driving up and down the Riviera Maya daily, but I have a more peaceful life now that my beautiful kids have become young adults and have left the nest.

After my challenges, my faith has led me to a new passion that I called SLIM SPA. Now helping clients feel better about themselves by improving their bodies and faces. And this has been such a blessing.

Faith is the biggest power on earth that often is forgotten. 

Believe and you can do the impossible!
