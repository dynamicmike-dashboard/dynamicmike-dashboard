Jelle de Jong Beltran
The Future of Healthcare

How Integral Diagnostics Drive True Healing

Many health issues are treated in isolation—one system at a time, one specialist at a time.

In this presentation, we explore why that traditional, monodisciplinary model often falls short when symptoms span multiple domains.

Physiotherapist and integral health strategist Jelle de Jong will introduce a diagnostic framework that connects body, mind, and biochemistry to uncover the real source of complex health problems.

Discover how integrated diagnostics can lead to deeper healing and more effective care.

Keith Commins
Achieve More Success By Breathing Less - The Overbreathing Epidemic

.

Keith is a long term digital nomad from Dublin, Ireland who discovered the power of breathing after suffering a lifetime from asthma, poor sleep and poor overall cardiovascular fitness.

In his talk today, he will explain how over breathing is fuelling a worldwide epidemic of poor health; and how breathing correctly can drastically improve focus, mood and cognition, athletic performance, sleep and overall health and well being.
