Who is Mike Berry?


Mike's Background

Some of you may already know Mike Berry as 'dynamicmike'.


Mike is the Founder and Chairman of the PDCYES group

  • Mike generated his first income online in 1996 (2 years before Google started).

  • He was the Founder & Chairman of YES-group Scotland in 2001 (originally started as a Tony Robbins & Personal Development support group).

  • Mike hosted the first-ever Internet Summit in Scotland (2004).

  • He has spoken on the Internet Platform around the world.

  • He created InspireScotland (2006) with the dream of Inspiring 10,000 people at once & he hired the largest conference centre in Scotland.

  • In 2008, Mike decided to "Walk The Talk" & he left Scotland in his campervan, taking in 21 countries with his dog & he continues to generate 100% of his income via the web.

  • Mike was involved in Mobile websites since inception & founded one of the Largest Mobile App Development businesses in UK (2012)

  • Starting with two computer qualifications in 1981, Mike has always enjoyed staying at the cutting edge of technology.

  • Mike has Mastered, written Advanced Guides & Video Tuition & still owns websites on Search Engine Optimisation

(SEO) and most of the major Social Media channels.

  • Ambassador for a special school in Belgium focusing on Highly Sensitive People (HSP)

  • Mike is an active member of the Rotary Club, Photographic Society and Speakers Association

  • An avid photographer, and insatiable adventurer, Mike has also hosted a weekly crypto mastermind chat since 2017.

  • Non-drinker, non-smoker, Kickboxing blackbelt with 18years of martial arts experience.

  • Mike is very much into positive vibrations, Law of Attraction, NLP and Loving Life Now!

  • Mike was the Founder & Chairman of YESgroup Scotland (a Tony Robbins & Personal Development support group), and with his Basket Brigade leadership, he was responsible for feeding 300+ needy families at Christmastime.

  • Mike has been involved in Personal Development since age 24 (he has been known as 'DynamicMike' since that time too), and he has attended (and spoken at) many seminars around the world, along with some amazing, inspirational leaders!

  • Mike was invited to HRH, Her Majesty The Queen's Royal Garden Party in recognition of his charitable efforts (at age 25, he was the youngest person there).

  • In 2006, Mike had the dream of inspiring 10,000 people at once, so he hired the largest conference centre in Scotland (SECC) and invested 1.5 years and all of his own fairly substantial savings in order to make it happen - and it did!! With Sir Bob Geldof as one of the main platform speakers.

  • Always keen to stay at the cutting edge of technology, Mike gained two computer qualifications (almost 40 years ago), and he has since written books, Advanced Guides & created Video Tuition Series & he owns websites on virtually every aspect of Social Media.

  • Mike has been involved in the Mobile Marketing business since inception, and he envisages some major breakthroughs continuing in our communication evolution through 2022 and beyond.

  • With over 30,000 hours studying Social Media, Mike is always delighted to share some of his knowledge with those who are ready to learn.
