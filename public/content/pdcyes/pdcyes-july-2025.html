Nikita Chakrabarty
Embodied Love & Conscious Relationships

.

Nita Chakrabarty is a trauma-informed relationship coach, international facilitator, and founder of the SoulEmbodiment™ Method, a transformational approach that helps high-performing men and women heal at the nervous system level, reclaim emotional power, and build deeply connected, conscious relationships.

Born in South Asia and having lived in seven countries, Nita is shaped by both ancient wisdom and modern leadership. She brings a rare fusion of heart, depth, and discernment to her work. After a career in tech and consulting, her own journey through trauma, burnout, and emotional shutdown led her to a deeper truth: love and success mean nothing without embodiment, intimacy, and inner safety.

Trained in over twenty modalities including somatic therapy, Breathwork, parks work , polarity work, shamanic healing, and Ayurvedic science, Nita’s coaching bridges science, spirit, and shadow. She guides visionary men to attract emotionally intelligent women and aligned romantic partners, and to evolve into the kind of partners, lovers, and leaders they secretly long to be.

Through private coaching, immersive retreats, and transformational workshops, Nita has helped thousands around the world break unhealthy patterns, awaken their depth, and create relationships rooted in truth, erotic polarity, and devotion.
