What is Personal Development?


Indeed Personal Development can mean many things to different people.

The vision for the PDC YES group is simply to learn, and discover and share; often information and strategies that we didn't even know that we didn't know. eg. Mindmapping, Speed-Reading, Chakras, Health & Nutrition etc

Sometimes the speakers may just be helping to remind us of some of the knowledge that we may already have, and sometimes just hearing it in a different way or at a different time, can be the catalyst to helping us take the next step in confidence.


Add it to your diary now - PDCYES group events happen only once per month, and are FREE to attend - don't miss out.
