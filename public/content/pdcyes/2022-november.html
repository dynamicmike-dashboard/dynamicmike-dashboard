Rianna & François - The 6 key elements to build and maintain strong relationships 

With nearly 8 billion people on the planet, communication is inevitable, and so is miscommunication. Mastering relationships is a skill you want to have. Rianna & François will share the 6 key elements to build and maintain strong relationships with the most important people in your life (friends, family, partners, and fellow travelers). 

Being partners in life & business for 8+ years, they’ve successfully made it through a long-distance relationship, speaking a different language, full-time traveling, doing business together, and overcoming traumas after a near-death experience and six years of infertility. 

These ups and downs taught them some valuable lessons they’d like to share with you.
