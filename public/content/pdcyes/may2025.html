Doron Hanoch
Dead for a Moment, Alive for Good: My Journey from Chaos to Inner Freedom


Finding Purpose, Breath, and Radical Aliveness in a World of Impermanence

What happens when life stops—literally?

In this powerful talk, Doron shares how a near-death experience in a New York elevator became the unexpected doorway to silence, purpose, and profound inner freedom.

A raw and inspiring journey that invites you to pause, breathe, and truly live.

Doron is a purpose-driven teacher, entrepreneur, and leader with over 30 years of experience in yoga, mindfulness, and holistic wellness. His story is not one of a straight path but of constant evolution—a journey through cultures, disciplines, and self-discovery.

Doron’s approach bridges tradition with modern life, depth with action, and wisdom with practicality.

He is now working on his seventh book, titled: “Undistracted,” on becoming a motivational speaker, a best-selling author, and a Joyful Jedi.

Shawn Wilson
The Key to Authentic Relationships

.

Discover how self-awareness is the key to living authentically in alignment with your mission, values, boundaries, and highest self in order to be able to manifest and create an authentic intimate relationship.

We’ll explore how unconscious self-betrayal, seeking love externally, and survival-based control patterns keep us from embodying true freedom, aligned relationships, and living from creation, and service to Source.

Introduction:

Shawn has been an authentic relating and men’s coach for 2 years. He has been trained by many of the worlds top leaders in these areas for 15+ years and he guides individuals into deep authenticity and embodied leadership by helping them align their relationships, mission, and self-worth to their authentic truth, to live in unity with the source within them in relationships, their mission, and themselves.
